WhatsApp has 500+ million active users in India. That's the adult population. Yet most Indian businesses use it like a glorified SMS channel. Here's how to do it properly in 2025.

## Why WhatsApp Beats Every Other Channel in India

| Channel | Open Rate | Click Rate | Reply Rate |
|---|---|---|---|
| Email | 18% | 2.5% | 0.5% |
| SMS | 45% | 4% | 1% |
| Push notifications | 20% | 3% | N/A |
| **WhatsApp (official API)** | **98%** | **45–60%** | **15–25%** |

One WhatsApp campaign outperforms 10 email blasts. The question is how to actually execute.

## The 4-Phase WhatsApp Marketing System

### Phase 1: Opt-In (Build a Clean List)
You **must** get explicit opt-in. No scraped numbers. No spam. Methods that work:

- Checkbox at checkout: "Send me order updates via WhatsApp"
- Lead magnet: "Download our pricing PDF via WhatsApp"
- QR codes in physical stores
- Website chat widget with WhatsApp option

**Target:** 40%+ opt-in rate on checkout.

### Phase 2: Templates (Get Them Approved)
Every proactive message from your business needs a **pre-approved template** from Meta. Categories:

- **Utility** — Order shipped, appointment reminder, OTP
- **Marketing** — Sale announcement, new product, abandoned cart
- **Authentication** — Login codes

Approval takes 24–48 hours. Rejections happen if templates look spammy or violate policy. Start with 5–10 core templates.

### Phase 3: Campaigns That Work in India

**Campaign 1: Order Lifecycle (Utility)**
- Order confirmed
- Order shipped (with tracking link)
- Out for delivery
- Delivered (with review request)

**Result:** 28% lower WISMO tickets (Where Is My Order).

**Campaign 2: Abandoned Cart (Marketing)**
- Hour 1: Cart reminder with "Complete Purchase" button
- Hour 6: 5% discount code
- Hour 24: Final nudge with social proof

**Result:** 15–22% cart recovery rate.

**Campaign 3: Festive Sales**
- Pre-sale teaser (3 days before)
- Sale-live blast
- Last-day reminder
- Post-sale "you missed it" recovery

**Result:** 3–5× higher conversion than email.

**Campaign 4: Re-engagement (30/60/90 days)**
- Personalized product recommendations
- Loyalty rewards
- Win-back discounts

**Result:** 12–18% repeat purchase rate uplift.

### Phase 4: Automation & AI
Manual WhatsApp doesn't scale past ~500 customers. You need:

- **Chatbot for replies** — answers FAQs 24/7, transfers hot leads to humans
- **Broadcast tools** — send to tens of thousands via approved templates
- **Segmentation** — target by purchase history, location, engagement
- **CRM sync** — Zoho, HubSpot, Salesforce integrations

## Metrics That Matter

Don't just track "messages sent." Track:

- **Opt-in rate** — % of checkout customers who opt in
- **Deliverability rate** — % of messages that arrive
- **Read rate** — % that are read (target 95%+)
- **Click rate** — % that click CTA (target 20%+)
- **Conversion rate** — % that complete the goal (target 8%+)
- **Cost per conversion** — total WhatsApp spend ÷ conversions
- **Opt-out rate** — keep below 1%

## Compliance Rules You Can't Ignore

1. Only message users who opted in
2. Respect the 24-hour free-form reply window
3. Mark marketing templates correctly
4. Honor opt-out requests within 24 hours
5. Don't use personal WhatsApp for business — use the **official API**

Meta will ban your number if you violate these. Recovery is painful.
