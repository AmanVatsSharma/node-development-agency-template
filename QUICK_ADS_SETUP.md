## üìû Emergency Troubleshooting

### "No impressions!"
‚Üí Check: Bid too low? Budget depleted? Keywords too specific?  
‚Üí Fix: Increase bids by 50%, check daily budget

### "High impressions, no clicks!"
‚Üí Check: Ad copy not compelling? Wrong audience?  
‚Üí Fix: Improve headlines, add price/offer, check locations

### "Clicks but no conversions!"
‚Üí Check: Landing page slow? Call button working? Form broken?  
‚Üí Fix: Test landing page, check phone number, verify conversion tracking

### "CPA too high!"
‚Üí Check: Wrong keywords? Broad match? Competition?  
‚Üí Fix: Pause expensive keywords, use exact match only, lower bids
