"How much does a good website design cost in India?" We get asked this daily. Here's the honest, unvarnished answer — broken down by type, quality, and what you actually get.

## Quick Price Reference

| Website Type | Cheap Template | Quality Custom | Premium Custom |
|---|---|---|---|
| Business website (5–10 pages) | ₹15,000 | ₹75,000–₹1,50,000 | ₹2,50,000+ |
| Corporate / agency | ₹35,000 | ₹1,25,000–₹2,50,000 | ₹4,00,000+ |
| E-commerce | ₹45,000 | ₹1,50,000–₹3,50,000 | ₹6,00,000+ |
| SaaS landing + app shell | ₹60,000 | ₹2,00,000–₹4,50,000 | ₹8,00,000+ |
| Enterprise platform | ₹1,50,000 | ₹6,00,000–₹15,00,000 | ₹25,00,000+ |

**What "cheap" really means:** a stolen template, poor SEO, no brand identity, no conversions. You're buying a graveyard.

**What "quality" means:** custom design by a real designer, conversion-focused UX, mobile-first, proper SEO, measurable results.

## The 5 Components of Website Cost

### 1. UX / Wireframing — 15% of budget
User flows, wireframes, information architecture. Skipping this is the #1 reason websites fail to convert.

### 2. Visual Design — 30% of budget
Custom Figma designs, brand identity, typography, color systems, illustrations, photography.

### 3. Development — 40% of budget
Converting designs to actual code, responsive layouts, animations, CMS integration, testing.

### 4. Content — 10% of budget
Copywriting, images, videos. Often overlooked — results in empty pages and weak SEO.

### 5. Technical Setup — 5% of budget
Hosting, domain, SSL, analytics, SEO setup, submission to Google Search Console.

## Template vs Custom — What You Actually Lose

Going with a template seems smart at first. Then reality hits:

| Area | Template | Custom |
|---|---|---|
| Brand differentiation | 0% | 100% |
| Load time | 4–6 seconds | 1–2 seconds |
| Mobile experience | Generic | Optimized |
| Conversion rate | 0.5–1.5% | 3–6% |
| SEO out of the box | Weak | Strong |
| Update safety | Breaks on theme updates | Rock solid |

A custom website at ₹2 lakh that converts at 4% earns 4–8× more than a ₹25,000 template at 1% — for the same traffic.

## What Drives Price Up (and When It's Worth It)

**Worth paying more for:**
- Custom Figma design (vs template customization) → ₹40,000–₹1,00,000 premium
- Next.js/React instead of WordPress → ₹50,000–₹1,50,000 premium
- SEO-first architecture with schema markup → ₹25,000–₹60,000 premium
- Real photography/video instead of stock → ₹30,000–₹1,50,000 premium
- Content writing by an actual copywriter → ₹25,000–₹75,000 premium

**Not worth paying more for:**
- Overpriced animations
- "AI-generated" code from white-label agencies
- 3D hero sections that wreck performance
- "Award-winning designs" that don't convert

## How to Budget Realistically

A useful rule: **budget 1–3% of your first year's projected online revenue** on your website.

- Target ₹50L online revenue? → Budget ₹50,000–₹1,50,000
- Target ₹2 Cr online revenue? → Budget ₹2,00,000–₹6,00,000
- Target ₹20 Cr online revenue? → Budget ₹20,00,000+

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What's the minimum I should spend on a professional website in India?**
₹75,000 is the practical floor for custom-designed, properly SEO'd, conversion-focused websites.

**Why is WordPress cheaper than Next.js?**
WordPress has more templates and easier customization. Next.js requires real developers but delivers far better SEO, speed, and long-term maintainability.

**How long does website design take in India?**
Simple business site: 3–4 weeks. Custom e-commerce: 6–10 weeks. SaaS platform: 10–16 weeks.

**Can I negotiate website design costs?**
Yes — reducing scope (fewer pages, fewer features) is the right way to reduce cost. Never negotiate on quality.
