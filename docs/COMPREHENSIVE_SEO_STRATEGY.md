## Strategy Components

### 1. On-Page SEO Optimization

#### H1 Tag Optimization
- **Rule**: One H1 per page with primary keyword
- **Format**: `[Primary Keyword] | [Service Name]`
- **Example**: "Hire Google Ads Expert | Professional Google Ads Management Services"

#### H2 Tag Optimization
- **Rule**: 3-5 H2 tags with secondary keywords
- **Format**: Natural headings that include keywords
- **Example**: "Google Ads Management Services", "PPC Management Solutions"

#### H3 Tag Optimization
- **Rule**: 5-10 H3 tags with long-tail keywords
- **Format**: Specific, descriptive headings
- **Example**: "Hire Google Ads Expert for Ecommerce"

#### Content Optimization
- **Keyword Density**: 1-2% for primary keyword
- **Semantic Keywords**: Use LSI keywords naturally
- **Content Length**: 1000+ words for service pages
- **Readability**: Flesch Reading Ease score 60+

### 2. Internal Linking Strategy

#### Link Structure
- **Hub Pages**: Main service pages (Google Ads, Web Development, etc.)
- **Spoke Pages**: Specific service pages (B2B Lead Gen, Next.js, etc.)
- **Related Pages**: Cross-link related services

#### Link Anchor Text
- **Primary Links**: Use primary keywords
- **Secondary Links**: Use secondary keywords
- **Natural Links**: Use descriptive text

#### Link Placement
- **Above the fold**: 1-2 internal links
- **Within content**: 3-5 internal links
- **Related services section**: 4-6 links
- **Footer**: Links to main services

### 3. Technical SEO

#### URL Structure
- **Format**: `/pages/[service-name]`
- **Keep**: Short, descriptive, keyword-rich
- **Avoid**: Parameters, unnecessary words

#### Site Speed
- **Target**: < 3 seconds load time
- **Optimize**: Images (WebP format)
- **Minimize**: JavaScript and CSS
- **Enable**: Compression and caching

#### Mobile Optimization
- **Responsive**: All pages mobile-friendly
- **Mobile-First**: Design for mobile first
- **Touch Targets**: Minimum 44x44px

#### Schema Markup
- **Service Schema**: All service pages
- **FAQ Schema**: Pages with FAQs
- **Breadcrumb Schema**: All pages
- **Review Schema**: Add for social proof
- **LocalBusiness Schema**: Location-based pages

### 4. Content Strategy

#### Content Types
1. **Service Pages**: Detailed service descriptions (1000+ words)
2. **Blog Posts**: Educational content (1500+ words)
3. **Case Studies**: Success stories (800+ words)
4. **FAQs**: Answer common questions
5. **Guides**: How-to guides and tutorials

#### Content Topics
- "How to [service]"
- "[Service] Best Practices"
- "[Service] vs [Alternative]"
- "[Service] Case Studies"
- "[Service] Pricing Guide"

#### Content Optimization
- **Keywords**: Primary, secondary, long-tail
- **Semantic Keywords**: LSI keywords throughout
- **Internal Links**: 3-5 per article
- **External Links**: 2-3 authoritative sources
- **Images**: Optimized with alt text

### 5. Local SEO (For Location-Based Services)

#### Google Business Profile
- **Optimize**: Complete profile with all details
- **Categories**: Relevant business categories
- **Photos**: High-quality business photos
- **Reviews**: Encourage customer reviews

#### Local Keywords
- **Format**: "[Service] in [City]"
- **Example**: "Web Development in Mumbai"
- **Pages**: Create location-specific pages

#### Local Citations
- **Directories**: List in relevant directories
- **Consistency**: NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency
- **Local Links**: Get links from local businesses

### 6. Link Building Strategy

#### Internal Links
- **Related Services**: Link to related services
- **Hub Pages**: Link to main service pages
- **Blog Posts**: Link to service pages
- **Footer**: Links to main services

#### External Links
- **Guest Posts**: Write for relevant blogs
- **Resource Pages**: Get listed on resource pages
- **Directories**: Submit to relevant directories
- **Partnerships**: Link exchange with partners

### 7. User Experience (UX) SEO

#### Page Speed
- **Optimize**: Images, JavaScript, CSS
- **CDN**: Use CDN for static assets
- **Caching**: Implement browser caching
- **Compression**: Enable Gzip compression

#### Mobile Experience
- **Responsive**: Mobile-friendly design
- **Touch**: Easy touch interactions
- **Navigation**: Easy mobile navigation
- **Forms**: Mobile-optimized forms

#### User Engagement
- **CTAs**: Clear call-to-actions
- **Forms**: Easy-to-use contact forms
- **Navigation**: Intuitive navigation
- **Content**: Engaging, valuable content
